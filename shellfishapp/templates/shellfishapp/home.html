Crustacean Resource Information System of India abbreviately termed as CrustaceaRIS facilitates the data management by the registered users. The data management section provides the ability to the registered user to feed the data into tables of the database, view the records and update the record by editing. This section includes the feature of deleting the records from the tables of the database. The interactive web-based form interfaces integrated with action command buttons, text boxes, drop down list boxes provide ease in manging both data and records. Such interfaces are available for shrimps and prawns, lobsters and crabs to manage the data at species level. This section also features to upload the images of species of shrimps and prawns, lobsters and crabs. Maximum three images can be uploaded using this feature. The database administrator has full control over the data entered by the registered user and can accept or reject the record entered by the user. The rejection of the record by the database administrator sends the record back to user with comments.
